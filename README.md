# 🏥 Health Insurance CRM Conversion Analysis

## 📌Project Overview

***
This project is an end-to-end Data Analytics case study focused on analyzing a Health Insurance CRM dataset to understand lead-to-customer conversion behavior. 
The goal is to identify key factors influencing conversions and provide actionable business insights.
***

## 🎯 Objective 

* Analyze lead conversion funnel
* Identify high-performing lead sources
* Understand customer segmentation
* Evaluate agent performance
* Optimize follow-up strategy


## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed
### 1. Conversion Funnel Analysis 
* Identified major drop-offs at the negotiation stage
* Conversion defined as: Closed Won
